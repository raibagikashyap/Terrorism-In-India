Terrorism In India

This website tries to tell shed light on the subject of Terrorism in India through the medium of numbers. The story has been divided into four parts

The first article tries to tell us what is the status of terrorism in India and where does the country stand as compared to other countries in this aspect.

The second article explains why in spite of the number of incidents being high in India, the number of people killed in total is less as compared to other countries

The third article explains the different types of terrorism in the Indian context which are a major threat in the current decade. It tries to explain in which part of India these different types of terrorists are active

The fourth article tries to analyze the current situation of terrorism in India and what the current government is doing about it. It tries to understand what experts have to say about all this.
